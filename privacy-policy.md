## 1. Introduction

Welcome to PupilNetwork ("we", "our", or "us"). PupilNetwork is a student study collaboration platform built and operated by an individual developer based in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at pupilnetwork.is-a.dev ("the Service").

By using PupilNetwork, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you provide directly
- **Account information:** Display name, username, email address, and password when you sign up.
- **Profile information:** Bio and profile details you choose to add.
- **Content:** Messages sent in study rooms, questions and answers posted on the Q&A board, and conversations with the AI Study Buddy.
- **Payment information:** When purchasing Premium, we collect your email address and UPI Transaction Reference (UTR) number. We do **not** collect or store any bank account details, UPI PIN, or card information.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, features used, rooms joined, and time spent on the platform.
- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, and IP address.
- **Cookies:** We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and session cookies for basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies.

### 2.3 Third-party services
- **Supabase:** We use Supabase for authentication and database storage. Your data is stored on Supabase's servers. See [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).
- **Google Gemini API:** Queries you send to the AI Study Buddy are processed by Google's Gemini API. Do not share sensitive personal information in AI queries. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Jitsi Meet:** Video calls use Jitsi's public infrastructure (meet.jit.si). We do not record or store video call content. See [Jitsi's Privacy Policy](https://jitsi.org/meet-jit-si-privacy/).
- **Resend:** We use Resend to send transactional emails (account confirmation, premium codes). See [resend.com/privacy](https://resend.com/privacy).
- **MongoDB Atlas:** AI conversation history is stored on MongoDB's servers. See [mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://www.mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account
- Provide the study rooms, Q&A board, and AI Study Buddy features
- Process Premium membership activations via the UPI code system
- Send transactional emails (welcome emails, premium codes)
- Display your username and points on the public leaderboard
- Improve the platform and fix bugs
- Detect and prevent abuse, spam, or fraudulent activity

We do **not**:
- Sell your data to any third party
- Use your data for advertising
- Share your data with any party except the third-party services listed above that are necessary to operate the platform

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share your data only in these limited circumstances:

- **With service providers** listed in Section 2.3, solely to operate the platform
- **Publicly:** Your username, display name, and points are visible to other users on the leaderboard and in study rooms. You control what you post.
- **Legal requirements:** If required by law, court order, or government authority in India

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time.
- **Chat messages:** Stored indefinitely to maintain room history. May be deleted upon request.
- **AI conversations:** Stored for up to 90 days, then automatically deleted.
- **AI cache:** Common query responses cached for 7 days to reduce API usage.
- **Premium codes:** Retained indefinitely for payment verification and fraud prevention.

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data by editing your profile
- **Delete** your account and associated data by emailing us
- **Object** to processing of your data
- **Data portability** — request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 7. Children's Privacy

PupilNetwork is intended for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

---

## 8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text (handled by Supabase Auth)
- All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
- The admin panel is protected by a secret key not accessible to users
- We do not store UPI PINs, bank details, or card numbers

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

- **No advertising or tracking cookies**

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may prevent you from logging in.
